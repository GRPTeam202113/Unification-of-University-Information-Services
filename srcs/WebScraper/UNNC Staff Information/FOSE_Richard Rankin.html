Qualifications

PhD in Mathematics from the University of Strathclyde (Glasgow, UK)

Biography

I joined UNNC in September 2017 after working at Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María (Valparaíso, Chile), Rice University (Houston, USA) and the University of Strathclyde (Glasgow, UK).

 Expertise Summary

Finite element methods for obtaining approximate solutions to partial differential equations
