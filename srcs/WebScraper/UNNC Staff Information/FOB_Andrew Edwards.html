Biography

Andrew Edwards joined UNNC to work in the CELE department in September 2006, moving to the International Business Division in 2008. Before teaching English he worked as an accountant for a number of years, and am an Associate of the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales. Andrew has experience of working in audit, internal audit, and accounting roles. In more recent years he has worked in language education, and shortly before coming to Ningbo took a Masters degree in Applied Linguistics.
