Biography

I have taught English for Academic Purposes since 1987. I arrived at CELE in 2018. I taught EAP at Pasadena City College (California, USA) for more than two decades and twice served as the program coordinator. From 2001 to 2005, I taught a range of classes in the field-based MA TESOL program at Azusa Pacific University. I have also served as an IELTS tester.

In addition to teaching my work in EAP, I have also worked in international higher education management, serving as the faculty lead/director of the International Student Pathway at Pasadena City College and facilitating international educational exchanges from 2013 to 2017.

Research interests

My academic interests include technology mediated second language acquisition, testing and assessment, curriculum/materials development, and language and identity formation.
