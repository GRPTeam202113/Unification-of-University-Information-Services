Biography

I joined UNNC in February 2018. Before that, I had spent my teaching career in various European countries including the UK where I worked at the University of Newcastle, the University of Edinburgh and Durham University as an EAP tutor. Apart from helping students develop their academic skills, I also have extensive experience teaching various exam classes (including IELTS) and Business English.

As my professional interests revolve around evaluation and assessment in education, I am also a member of the Assessment Development Team.

I spend my spare time doing martial arts (Brazilian jiu-jitsu), learning foreign languages and reading widely about science, philosophy and psychology.


Teaching

Reading and Writing in Academic Contexts; Listening and Speaking in Academic Contexts; English for Specific Academic Contexts (International Business and Computer Science); Academic Oral Presentations; Knowledge Exchange classes (IELTS, Business English, teacher training).
