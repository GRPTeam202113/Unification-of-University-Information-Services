Biography

I joined UNNC in September 2016, having previously worked for the University of London (Queen Mary and Royal Holloway) for two years, where I taught on a variety of EAP programmes (pre-sessional, in-sessional and pre-masters). Prior to teaching at the University of London, I taught ESOL in east London for five years, preceded by a year’s spell of working as a graduate trainee  teacher in a state school in Essex UK.

At CELE, this is my second year strand-leading the English in Specific Academic Contexts (Computer Science) course.
