Biography

Donglei Sun joined the University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC) in September 2020 as a preliminary year tutor in Mathematics.

Before joining UNNC, Donglei worked as a graduate research assistant at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), where he also served as a teaching assistant for several courses, including Introduction to Aerospace Engineering, Computer Aided Design, and Aerospace Control Systems. He also worked as an educational technology specialist for a year at Applied Technologies for Learning in Arts and Sciences in the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences, UIUC, where he provided technical support to instructors of online courses.

Donglei’s research interests include flight control systems, adaptive control theory, and robotics. He is a member of the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics.
