Biography

Dr Xiaoling Liu graduated from The University of Nottingham and joined Ningbo campus (UNNC) in 2015. Within only a little over 2 years, Dr Liu has built up a research platform from scratch to train undergraduate students, PhDs and support industry with 2 key labs, collaborating a joint lab with AVIC Composite group, she has recruited more than 10 researchers and secured in excess of RMB 25M to date from multiple sources, including famous international companies as well as prestigious state-level SoEs.

Dr Liu has held visiting fellowships at prestigious institutions in the region – NUS and National Tsinghua University. She currently focus on the research of polymer composites, especially the functional (biomedical, fire retardant, conductive) and sustainable (green, thermoplastic, recycling) composites; managing 7 major research projects, including a Zhejiang Provincial Innovation team and a MOST granted international base; and taking UNNC composites technology from start-up status to national recognition within China’s fast-moving composites industry.
