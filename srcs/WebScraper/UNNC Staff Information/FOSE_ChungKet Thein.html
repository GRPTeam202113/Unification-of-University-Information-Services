Biography

Expertise Summary

Dr ChungKet Thein is an Assistance Professor at School of Aerospace Engineering at UNNC. He obtained his PhD from University of Hull, UK, in the area of structural optimisation in various applications. His main research interests include the vibration energy harvesting design (both electromagnetic and piezoelectric), multi-objective and multidisciplinary structural optimisation using finite element analysis. He currently focuses on the electromagnetic vibration energy harvesting design and applications, composite materials and application of energy harvesting.
