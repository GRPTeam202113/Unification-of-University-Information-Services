Biography

I began my career as an English Instructor in 2009. Since then I have been instructing both academic and general English courses at universities and public schools in South Korea, Saudi Arabia, and most recently China. I began working at UNNC in July 2017. My academic focuses are in sociolinguistics, corpus linguistics and corpus-assisted-discourse-studies (CADS), as well as second language acquisition – with a particular interest in learner autonomy and feedback. I hope to begin a PHD in the near future.
