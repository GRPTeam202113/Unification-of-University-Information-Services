Biography

My first career was in Fashion Buying and Merchandising in London. I began English Language Teaching in 2009 and I began teaching EAP in 2011. In addition to teaching, I run the Extensive Reading provision for CELE students, ‘Reading Circles’ and have developed an NAA module which allows year 2-4 UNNC students to work with CELE tutors in running the ‘Reading Circles’ programme.  


Teaching

Undergraduate EAP, RWAC, LSAC, AOP, ESACa International Business.

 


Research interests

My research interests include; Extensive Reading, Student Mediation, Student Engagement and Interaction, Technology in the Classroom, Clickers and Online/Audience Response Systems and Student Feedback.
