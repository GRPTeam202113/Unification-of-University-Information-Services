Biography

Dr Lina Zhang is a Teaching Fellow in Management Science at Nottingham University Business School (NUBS) China, University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC). She is very passionate about research on how contemporary retailers revise and reconfigure their supply chains in the transformation to Omni-channel retailing. She is also keen on digital supply chain, green supply chain and supply chain network design.
