Biography

Lefan is currently a preliminary year tutor in Business/Economics at CELE, UNNC. She obtained her PhD in Economics from School of Economics, University of Nottingham in the UK in 2016.

Research interests

Lefan’s PhD thesis examines the extent to which the elderly in Chinese households are able to manage the burden of chronic diseases and disabilities using data from the China Health and Retirement Longitudinal Study (CHARLS). 

Research Interests

Applied Microeconometrics, Development Economics, Health Economics and Public Economics
