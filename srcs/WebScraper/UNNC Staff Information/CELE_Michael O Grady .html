Biography

I have extensive materials design experience- having created the Reading and Vocabulary Enhancement module and all associated examinations. I have previous experience of designing ESP courses for 4th year undergraduates in Chemistry, Physics and Financial Mathematics. 


Teaching

I teach on CELE Content modules Critical Thinking in Academic Contexts ( CTAC) and Reading and Vocabulary Enhancement (RAVE). I also work for The Writing Lab, with responsibility for the Faculty of Science and Engineering, running bespoke workshops for individual schools and advising extensively in one to one settings.
