Biography

I have taught in six different countries around the world. Apart from China and the UK, I have also worked in Croatia, Turkey, Saudi Arabia and South Korea. Ningbo is now the fourth Chinese city that I have lived and worked in. Previously, I taught in Guangzhou, Dalian and Suzhou. In addition to teaching EAP for Business, Science, Mathematics and Art & Design, I have considerable experience in classroom materials design. 

Teaching is my second career. From 1983 to 1993, I worked as a graphic designer within the advertising industry in the UK. I continue to draw and paint as a hobby and have a particular interest in drawing portraits. 


Teaching

RWAC, OSCa, OCSb, ESACb Maths, Introduction to History


Research interests

Academic interests include classroom task design, the teaching of intonation in academic settings and the development of learner training and metacognition.
