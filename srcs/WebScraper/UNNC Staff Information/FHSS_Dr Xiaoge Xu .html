Biography

Before joining the University of Nottingham Ningbo China in July 2018, Dr. Xu worked at Xiamen University Malaysia, Botswana International University of Science and Technology, University of Nottingham Ningbo China, Nanyang Technological University in Singapore, City University of Hong Kong, and China School of Journalism.

Research interests

Expertise Summary

Dr. Xu’s major academic interests and expertise lie largely in mobile, creative and experience studies. As the founder of Mobile Studies International, Dr. Xu has been passionately advocating and promoting mobile studies around the world. On top of that, he has also been working as a media and communication consultant for various organizations in Asia and beyond.  

As the founding director of Institute for Mobile Studies at the School of International Communication, Dr. Xu has initiated a few major projects related to mobile studies, including annual events such as Mobile Studies Summer School, International Mobile Storytelling Congress, and Mobile Studies Congress.

Dr. Xu has been staying closely connected with mobile industries for collaboration and consultancy. Since 2013, he has been serving as the editor-in-chief of an IGI Global book series Advances in Wireless Technologies and Telecommunications (AWTT), which has published 76 volumes. 

Research Interest

Mobile studies, journalism studies, media and communication studies
