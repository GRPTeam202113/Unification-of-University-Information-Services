Biography

Dr. Lin Huang joined Nottingham University Business School as a teaching fellow in Entrepreneurship in February 2017.

Dr. Lin Huang obtained her PhD in Business from University of Greenwich (UK), and her research focuses on supply chain management, especially in supply chain collaboration. Lin worked on a project explored the conﬁguration patterns of dyadic and triadic collaborations in supply chain network through cases in diﬀerent industries.

Dr. Lin Huang is an accredited fellow of the Higher Education Academy and was granted the Postgraduate Certificate in Higher Education (PGCHE) in 2020.

Research interests

Dr. Lin Huang’s research interests include supply chain management, business model development and company growth. Dr. Lin Huang is also conducting research on entrepreneurial career choice, to explore the influence of religion and culture on the choices of individuals. In addition, Dr. Lin Huang is actively engaged in research related to entrepreneurship education, she is exploring how to use project-based learning (PBL) method to grow students’ entrepreneurial intention as well as their learning outcomes in entrepreneurship relevant courses.
