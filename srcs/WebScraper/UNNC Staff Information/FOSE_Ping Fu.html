Biography

Expertise Summary

My research focuses on understanding how glacier changes in response to climate change at different time scales and how glacier shapes earth surface. I am using multiple approaches including geomorphic mapping techniques, cosmogenic dating methods, and numeric modelling to reconstruct glaciations and their impacts on landscape evolution. I am also interested in monitoring recent glacier change from satellite images and drone images.
