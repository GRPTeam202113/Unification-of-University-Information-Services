Biography

My previous teaching experience was with Chinese EFL learners of different proficiencies. During my undergraduate years, I volunteered in teaching English to senior high school students in Guizhou Province in two of my summer vacations, and during my post-graduate years, I worked as interim or substitute College English at various public Chinese universities, including Communication University of Zhejiang (previously named as 'Zhejiang University of Media and Communications'). Besides, I was involved in marking CET-3/4/6 exam papers twice a year for 3 years. Both my dissertations for the bachelor’s and the master’s degree focused on Chinese EFL learners and their stages in acquiring a foreign language. 


Teaching

Preliminary year EAP courses, including Reading and Writing in Academic Contexts, Oral Academic Communication Skills, English for Specific Academic Contexts.
