Biography

I started teaching English in 2010, beginning with General English in Shanghai and later in York, England. Before joining UNNC in September 2017, I was teaching undergraduates Academic English for two years at Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University in Suzhou.


Teaching

RWAC (Reading and Writing in Academic Contexts)

LSAC (Listening and Speaking in Academic Contexts)

ESAC (English in Specific Academic Contexts: International Business)

AOP (Academic Oral Presentations)


Research interests

My MA dissertation focused on pragmatic transfer between EFL learners’ L1 pragmatic knowledge and their L2 realisation of speech acts.
