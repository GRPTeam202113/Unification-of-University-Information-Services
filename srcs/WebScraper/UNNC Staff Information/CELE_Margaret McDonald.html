Biography

Started teaching career as supply teacher in secondary schools and FE colleges in UK.  Worked for VSO in Ethiopia as a trainer of trainers and teachers in modern teaching methods.  Taught EAP in summer pre-sessionals at Heriot Watt University in Edinburgh and Leicester University.  Taught EAP, Introduction to UK legal system and government and politics to pre-sessional and foundation students at University of Huddersfield for 3 years.
