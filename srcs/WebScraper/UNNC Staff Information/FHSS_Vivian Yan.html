Biography

I graduated from The Chinese University of Hong Kongwith a master degree of Chinese Linguistics and Language Acquisition in 2012.

I worked for Columbia University for two months to teach business Mandarin for students in the business Chinese and internship program. I taught students of intermediate level and advanced level Comprehensive Business Chinese.

At the end of 2012, I joined the University of Nottingham Ningbo China. At UNNC, I am module convenors of credit-based Mandarin modules and no credit-based Mandarin modules. I am also taking the role of Commercial programs manager of the Language centre and responsible for running Mandarin Summer school and Commercial evening classes for all languages(except Mandarin).
