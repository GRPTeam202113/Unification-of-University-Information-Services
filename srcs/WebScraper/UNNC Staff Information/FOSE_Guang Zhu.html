Biography

Dr. Zhu’s research mainly focuses on the fundamentals and applications of functional flexible materials their uses in flexible transducers. He has published 50 first-authored and correspondence-authored research papers on internationally prestigious journals such as Nat. Commun.. Adv. Mater., and Nano Lett. Total publication citations by 11,000 times with average impact factor of 12 and H-index of 51. He has granted with 6 US patents and 25 Chinese patents. His research achievements have been highlighted by the top journals including Science and Nature, and reported by public media such as CNN, Reuters, CCTV, and Chinese Science News.
