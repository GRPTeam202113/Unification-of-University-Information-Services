Biography

Dr. Yetaotao Qiu is currently an Assistant Professor of Accounting at Nottingham University Business School China. She obtained her PhD degree in Accounting from Concordia University John Molson School of Business in 2020. Dr. Qiu’s research interests include corporate governance, corporate disclosure, product market competition, textual analysis, and inter-firm relations and information externalities. She has presented her work in regional seminars as well as leading international conferences such as American Accounting Association Annual Conference, Canadian Academic Accounting Association Annual Conference, and so on. She has also worked as the Co-Investigator for multiple research grants such as CAAA Research Grant and CASA Research Funding.
