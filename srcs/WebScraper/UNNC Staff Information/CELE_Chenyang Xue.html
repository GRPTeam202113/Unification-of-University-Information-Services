Biography

Chenyang Xue is a preliminary year Mathematics tutor at CELE since 2021. Prior to joining CELE, he obtained Ph.D. degree in 2020 at University of Nottingham, Ningbo China and worked as a research fellow in multiphase flow as postdoctoral practical training in UNNC.


Teaching

Foundation Algebra for Physical Sciences & Engineering


Research interests

His research is focusing on flow visualization of chemical reactors and computational fluid dynamics of multiphase flows.
