Biography

I joined UNNC in September 2018, but before this, I taught EAP and ESP in Dalian Polytechnic University, in the north of China. As well as this, I spent about 15 years teaching General and Business English in Eastern Europe: Poland, Russia and Ukraine. My experience includes 12 years working in Ukraine, where I spent a large part of this time organizing training sessions for primary and secondary teachers there.
