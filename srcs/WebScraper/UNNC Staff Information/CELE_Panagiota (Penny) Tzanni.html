Biography

After graduating from the University of Birmingham, Penny Tzanni worked primarily in universities around the world teaching English for Academic Purposes. She has taught in Kuwait, United Arab Emirates, United Kingdom and China and  as a Teaching Fellow at University College London, teaching mostly postgraduate students. Her primary research focus is feedback and peer feedback in the EAP classroom.
