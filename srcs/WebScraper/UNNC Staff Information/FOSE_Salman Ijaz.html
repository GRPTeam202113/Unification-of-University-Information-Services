Biography

I received a Ph.D. Degree in Control Sciences and Control Engineering from Beihang University (BUAA) Beijing in 2018. I then joined Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA) to pursue post-doctoral studies where I worked for two years. I am currently serving as an Assistant Professor in the School of Aerospace, University of Nottingham Ningbo China. My research area includes the modeling and fault-tolerant control of aircraft systems, sliding mode control, control allocations, robust control, and unmanned aerial vehicles. I have three years of teaching experience in different public and private sectors universities.
