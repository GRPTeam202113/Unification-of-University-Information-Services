Biography

Shanshan QU is a preliminary year Business/Economics tutor at CELE since 2021. Prior to joining CELE, she obtained Ph.D. degree in 2021 at Nottingham Business School. Now, she is the module convenor of 'Introduction to Statistics for Business and Economics' (CELEF009) and tutor of 'Introduction to Mathematics for Business and Economics' (CELEF010).

Shanshan's research interests include empirical asset pricing, market anomalies, and liquidity risks.
