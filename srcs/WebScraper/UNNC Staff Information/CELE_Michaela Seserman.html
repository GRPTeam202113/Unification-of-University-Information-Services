Biography

I have been teaching since 2005, in India, Turkey, the UK and China. I started teaching English for Academic Purposes in 2010. Prior to joining CELE in October 2019, I worked at the University of Liverpool, UK, where I taught on the in-sessional and pre-sessional provisions.


Teaching

Undergraduate EAP

Summer Pre-sessional


Research interests

Mobile Assisted Language Learning (MALL), using technology in pronunciation practice, grammar for academic purposes.
