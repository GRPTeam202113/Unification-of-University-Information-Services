Biography

I joined UNNC in September 2018. Prior to this I was an associate teacher at the University of Nottingham International College in Nottingham (UK) and a PhD student in the quantum gravity group in the School of Mathematical Sciences at the University of Nottingham, UK. 

Expertise Summary

Mathematical and theoretical physics, curved spacetime quantum field theory and its applications to cosmology and black holes, particle detectors, fundamental aspects of quantum theory


Teaching

Vector Calculus (MATH2032)

Analytical and Computational Foundations (MATH1028)

Probability (MATH1032)


Research interests

Mathematical and theoretical physics, quantum field theory in curved spacetimes, quantum field theory application to non-linear quantum optics, foundation of quantum mechanics, quantum gravity.
