Biography

Dr. Dimple Thadani was born and grew up in Hong Kong. She is currently an Assistant Professor in Information Systems at Nottingham University Business School (NUBS) China. Prior to joining UNNC, Dimple was a lecturer (School of Business) and an administrative staff (Teaching and Learning Centre) at Hong Kong Baptist University. Dimple received her PhD from City University of Hong Kong in 2013. Her research interests include social media, leadership and online collaborative games, e-commerce, and e-learning. She has published in international journals and leading information systems conference proceedings. Dimple received the best PhD Student Award at the 4th World Summit on the Knowledge Society.
