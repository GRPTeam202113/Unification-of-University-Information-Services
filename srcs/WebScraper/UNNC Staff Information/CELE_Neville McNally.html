Biography

I have previously taught General English in language schools in Taiwan and Japan. I taught for one semester each at North China University of Science and Technology (Taiyuan, Shanxi) and Shenzhen University. I have taught EAP at Sino-British College in Shanghai, and on two pre-sessionals in the UK, at Queen’s University Belfast and Southampton University.

I joined UNNC in December 2008 where I taught Oral Communication Skills and Written Communication Skills, which were the modules that preceded RWAC, LSAC, ESAC and AOP. I have also been involved in developing and teaching on the CELE-KE IELTS course, and on the UNNC summer pre-sessional.
