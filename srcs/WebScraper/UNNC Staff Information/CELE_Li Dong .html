Biography

With a teaching and research focus on international business, management and economy, I hold a Postgraduate Certificate in Academic Practice in Teaching and Learning, and am a Fellow at Higher Education Academy in the UK. I have been teaching at undergraduate and postgraduate levels in China, Singapore, and the UK for the past ten years.
