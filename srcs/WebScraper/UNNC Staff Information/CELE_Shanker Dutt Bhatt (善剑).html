Biography

Mr Bhatt has over twenty years of teaching and leadership experience. He has been instrumental in the development of internationalised education for Chinese students for about 17 years.

He was the Founding Principal at the International Curriculum Centre of the High School Affiliated to Renmin University of China (Renda Fuzhong), Nanjing Jinling High School and of the International Division at Suzhou International Academy affiliated to Beijing Foreign Studies University (Beiwai Suzhou Fuxiao). He was also the Academic Principal at the Beijing Luhe International Academy.

Prior to embarking on the journey of Middle and High school education, Mr Bhatt worked for various universities as a lecturer, program manager and researcher such as University of South Australia, University of London External programs, Gujarat University and IIT Mumbai.


Research interests

His research interests include Educational Leadership, Building Sustainable Schools, Self-seeking, Fear and Creativity. When time permits - and it always permits - he likes to be close to the existence in the form of children and nature, loves reading about existentialism, writes poems and plays badminton. His educational philosophy is much influenced by J. Krishnamurti, which revolves around the true meaning of educating the child for facing oneself and the world as it is, rather what it should be!
