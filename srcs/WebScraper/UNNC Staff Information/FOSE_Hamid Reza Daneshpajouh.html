Biography

I joined UNNC in September 2021, before that I was a postdoctoral researcher in CERMICS research centre at Ecole des Ponts ParisTech hosted by Prof. Frédéric Meunier . Prior to that, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Institute for Research in Fundamental Sciences, and a researcher at Moscow Institute of Physics and Technology.


Teaching

Discrete Mathematics and Graph Theory (2022)

Game Theory (2022)

Coding and Cryptography (2021)


Research interests

Broadly speaking, my research interests lies in the intersection of combinatorics and topology. In particular, using topological and geometric techniques to study combinatorial problems.


Honorary positions

Research and travel grants, awarded by Iranian National Elites Foundation (9000$, 2015-2017).

Ranked 6th in the National University Entrance Exam for PhD in Mathematics (2012).

Silver Medals, 32th Iranian National Mathematics Competition for University Students (2008).
