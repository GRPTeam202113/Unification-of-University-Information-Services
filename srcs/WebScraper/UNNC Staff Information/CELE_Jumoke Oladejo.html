Biography

Dr. Jumoke Oladejo (Prefers being called Jummy) was awarded a PhD in Energy Technologies from the Department of Chemical and Environmental Engineering at the University of Nottingham, Ningbo China (UNNC) in July 2018. During her research degree, she was also a teaching assistant at UNNC, helping with undergraduate seminars and laboratory sessions, i.e., Preliminary year foundation science, 2nd year Chemical Engineering Process Control and 2nd year Electrical and Electronics Engineering Laboratory. As part of her duties as a teaching assistant, she supervised laboratory sessions, marked laboratory reports and gave constructive feedback to the students. In addition to this, she was an hall tutor with UNNC Residential college for providing pastoral care and a support system to all students for enhancing their experience on campus. In February 2019, She joined UNNC as a Preliminary Year Tutor in Science and Engineering.
