Biography

I joined The University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC) as Senior Tutor in Science and Engineering – Mathematics in March 2013, having previously worked as Mathematics Coordinator at UCL Centre for Preparatory Studies in Astana, Kazakhstan.

Prior to working at UCL, I worked at The University of Nottingham, UK, as Lecturer in Applied Mathematics during 2005-2009, where I taught Honours Mathematics and Service Teaching from Foundation Year, Qualifying Year and Second Year.

In addition to working in the UK, China and Kazakhstan, I have worked for over 14 years in India, which include position at National Institute of Technology, and also delivered talks in Italy and Portugal.
