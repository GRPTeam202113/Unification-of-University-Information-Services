Biography

I have been an educator since 2000. The core idea of my teaching philosophyis to enhance the student experience and support their learning in different environments. I understand education as an holistic process that embroils practice, reflection and knowledege. I believe in education as a pathway for change and development. I have a vast experience working with Asian students, supporting them to develop their critical thinking and to shape their profile as global citizens.
