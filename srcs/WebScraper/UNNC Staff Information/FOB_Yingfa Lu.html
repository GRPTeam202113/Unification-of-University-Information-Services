Biography

Yingfa Lu studied at Nankai, Nanjing and Edinburgh universities. He taught at the University of Bristol and was a visiting fellow at the University of Oxford. Yingfa is a CPA and qualified in law. Yingfa Lu's research focuses on auditing, accounting and corporate governance, particularly audit quality, regulation, accounting profession, legal liability of business professionals, and accounting history. He uses both qualitative and quantitative methods, and is also interested in trying new methodology, such conducting experiment and making use of biometers. Yingfa welcomes inquiries on PhD study or research collaboration. 


Teaching

Yingfa teaches auditing, financial accounting, financial management, and management accounting at both UG and PG levels.
