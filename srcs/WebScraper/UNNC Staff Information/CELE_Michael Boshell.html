Biography

I have taught EAP/EFL for a number of years in the following countries: Spain, Saudi Arabia, UAE and China. I have also held other positions such as programme coordinator, curriculum leader and module convener/team leader throughout my career. I was also an IELTS examiner for 14 years. My interests include running, playing football and watching TV series.
