Qualifications

Xiaoquan Liu a Professor in Finance. Prior to joining the University of Nottingham Ningbo in September 2014, she was Lecturer and Senior Lecturer in Finance with Essex Business School, University of Essex, UK. Xiaoquan has also held visiting positions in the Stephen M. Ross School of Business of the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, and the Department of Finance and Statistics of the University of Science and Technology of China. Her research is mainly in the areas of asset pricing, derivatives, and applied financial econometrics. She has published a number of articles in leading international journals, including the Journal of Economic Dynamics and Control, the Journal of Banking and Finance, and the European Journal of Operational Research. She has taught a range of modules at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels. These include asset pricing, derivative securities, risk management, financial intermediaries, and quantitative methods. Xiaoquan has also supervised a number of PhD students to completion in the broad areas of option pricing, financial engineering, and credit risk modeling.
