Biography

Yang joined the school of Economics in 2020 as an Assistant Professor at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China.  She received her Ph.D. in Economics from McGill University in 2020. Her research interests include Health Economics, Industrial Organization, Applied Econometrics. Her research uses rigorous empirical analysis to evaluate public policies, especially health care policies.
