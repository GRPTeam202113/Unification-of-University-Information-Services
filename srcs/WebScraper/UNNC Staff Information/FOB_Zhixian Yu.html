Biography

Dr. Yu is currently a Teaching Fellow in the University of Nottingham Ningbo China’s Business School. His main research interest is microeconomic theory, game theory and bargaining theory. His interest also extends to behavioral theory and information transmission. Dr. Yu obtained a Bachelor degree in Economics from NJU, Master degrees from UCL and TSE, followed by a Ph.D. in Economics from University of Nottingham.
