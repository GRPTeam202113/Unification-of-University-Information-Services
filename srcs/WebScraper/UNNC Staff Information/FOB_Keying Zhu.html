Biography

Dr Keying Zhu is a teaching fellow in accounting at Nottingham University Business School (NUBS) China. She teaches multiple courses at different levels for both lectures and seminars. She has also participated in several research projects. In addition to her teaching and research, she also works as exchange coordinator in the North American area.

Prior to joining NUBS, she worked in PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC), and trained as an independent auditor. She has participated in many listed firms’ annual audit and has also audited several IPO projects.
