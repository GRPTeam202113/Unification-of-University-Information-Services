Biography

I have been in English education since 2010, teaching a wide variety of modules and content in the United States, Japan, and China. I’ve been with UNNC since 2013.

Programs & Roles

I currently manage the Writing Lab@UNNC which provides university-wide in-sessional student support and programs for Preliminary Year through Post Graduate studies; I also manage the CELE International Internship Programme.


Teaching

Advanced English Discourse Skills-module convener (post graduate), Reading and Writing in Academic Contexts (EAP), Listening and Speaking in Academic Contexts (EAP), International Studies (EAP), International Business (EAP), Academic Oral Presentations (EAP), Introduction to Academic Skills (EAP), Introduction to Analytical Thought (EAP) 


Conference presentations

Stauffer, M. & O’Connell, J. (2019). ‘Support for Failing Students in an EAP Preliminary Year Programme: A Pilot’. Asia TEFL International: ELT for Glocal Synergies Across Disciplines and Multilingual Ambiences. 27- 29 June, Bangkok, Thailand.

 

Stauffer, M (2018). ‘Challenges & Solutions for EAP In-sessional Support in a Growing Sino-British University’. EAP Ireland: EAP as a Learning Community: New Directions and Opportunities. 6 October, Dublin, Ireland.

 

Stauffer, M. (2016). ‘EAP & International Communications: Challenges for Media Studies Curriculum in Asia’. TESOL Indonesia International Conference: Teaching & Learning English in Indonesia – Future Trends and Approaches. University of Mataram, 11-13 August Lombok, Indonesia.
