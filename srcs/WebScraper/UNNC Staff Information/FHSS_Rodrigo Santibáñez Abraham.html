Biography

I´ve taught Spanish in Chile and China since 2014. Since I came to China in 2017, before joining UNNC, I taught at Ningbo Polytechnic and Beijing Business and Technology University.

This year (2020-2021), I´m the convener of Spanish Stage 1 and 4. I´m also the Siele Exam Coordinator at the University.

I organize my classes from the communicative approach putting the students as the core of them. I´m constantly looking for new ways of teaching and update my knowledge. My goal is always to offer high-quality classes that focus on facilitating the student´s learning and development.
