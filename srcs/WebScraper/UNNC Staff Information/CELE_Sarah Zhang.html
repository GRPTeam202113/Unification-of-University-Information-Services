Biography

I started off as an English tutor 15 years ago at Ningbo University where I taught such courses as English Reading and Writing for English majors. From 2011 to 2018, I have spear-headed and completed five funded research projects on learning autonomy, formulaic sequences and teaching English writing.


Research interests

formulaic sequences; teaching English writing
