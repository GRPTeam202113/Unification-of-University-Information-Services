Biography

Teaching background

I joined The University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC) in February 2013 and teach EAP and ESP to undergraduate students. I previously taught EAP to international students at the University of New South Wales in Sydney, Australia and at Kaplan Higher Education in Chengdu, China. Prior to moving into EAP teaching, I worked as a Telecommunications Engineer and have twenty-two years of corporate work experience in Australia.

Specialisation

I am the founder of the Study Skills Program in Australia and I specialise in teaching study and life skills to young adults. Through this programme I was able to inspire, motivate and teach study and life skills to hundreds of students from public and private high schools in Australia.

Research interests

I am interested in researching topics on motivation and understanding difficulties Asian students face when they enter western learning environment.
