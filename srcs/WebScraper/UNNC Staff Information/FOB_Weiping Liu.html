Biography

Dr Weiping Liu is an assistant professor in international business and strategy at Nottingham University Business School China. Dr Liu’s principal research interests focus on issues within the fields of international business, strategic management, and CSR. She has published articles in journals such as Industrial Marketing Management, Management International Review, Management Decision, Journal of Business Research, Asia Pacific Journal of Management, and Journal of Hospitality & Tourism Research.
