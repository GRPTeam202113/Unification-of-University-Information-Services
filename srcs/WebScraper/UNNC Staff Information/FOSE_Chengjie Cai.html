Biography

Chengjie Cai joined the University of Nottingham Ningbo China in Jan 2019 as an Teaching Fellow in Applied Mathematics/Statistics. He received her BSc degrees from both Donghua University and University of Strathclyde through a 2+2 programme in 2015. Then he obtained his PhD in 2020, fully funded by China Scholarship Council and University of Strathclyde.
