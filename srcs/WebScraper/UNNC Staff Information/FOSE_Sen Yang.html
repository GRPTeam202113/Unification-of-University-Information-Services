Biography

Sen YANG joined University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC) in September 2020 as Teaching Fellow in Applied Mathematics and transferred to Electrical and Electronic Engineering in February 2022 as Assistant Professor.

Research interests

Cuff-less non-invasive blood pressure estimation using PPG and ECG signals with the aid of machine learning algorithms
