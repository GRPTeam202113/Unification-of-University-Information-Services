Biography

Sen YANG joined University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC) in September 2020 as a Teaching Fellow in Applied Mathematics.


Teaching

Currently

  • Mathematical Methods for Civil Engineering (MATH1031)
  • Mathematics for Engineers (MMME1042)
  • Mathematical Methods for Architectural and Environmental Engineering (MATH1046)
  • Mathematical Methods for Chemical and Environmental Engineering (MATH1047)
  • Analytical and Computational Foundations (MATH1028)

Previously

  • Foundation Calculus and Mathematical Techniques (CELEN037)
  • Mathematical Software (CELEN085)

Research interests

Cuff-less non-invasive blood pressure estimation using PPG and ECG signals with the aid of machine learning algorithms


Awards / Patents

Publications
