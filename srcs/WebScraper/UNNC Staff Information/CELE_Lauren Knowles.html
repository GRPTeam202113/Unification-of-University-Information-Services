Biography

Lauren Knowles is an EAP tutor at the University of Nottingham Ningbo. Prior to this post, she taught English in Spain, Italy, Argentina and Qatar where she completed a Graduate Diploma in English Language Teaching and trained as a teacher trainer. She then specialised in English for Academic Purposes during her employment at Newcastle University and developed an interest in English for Science and Engineering. Now at UNNC she co-convenes the English for Engineering module for preliminary year students and is completing an MA in English Linguistics.
