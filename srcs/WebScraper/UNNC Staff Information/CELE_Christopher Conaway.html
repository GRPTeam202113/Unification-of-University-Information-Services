Biography

Like many English language educators, I started as a personal tutor during college. After obtaining my BA, I spent a year teaching at a Korean hakwon in Seoul. I then obtained my MA and moved to China, teaching at a number of universities, including Chongqing University and Wenzhou-Kean University. I have also spent time as a full time "Language Assessment Consultant" in Shanghai.
