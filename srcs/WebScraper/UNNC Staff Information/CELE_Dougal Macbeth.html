Biography

I have been teaching EAP at the University of Nottingham, Ningbo since March, 2016.  Before this, I taught EAP and General English at various universities in Dongbei and Shandong, including key national universities (Project 211 and 985).  I have also taught Medical English, Business English and Business skills corporately.  I have spent most of my life in Canada and New Zealand, but I love travelling, learning languages, and immersing myself in different cultures.


Teaching

Undergraduate EAP including Reading and Writing in Academic Contexts, Listening and Speaking in Academic Contexts, Academic Oral Presentations, Introduction to Analytical Thought, English for Specific Academic Contexts.
