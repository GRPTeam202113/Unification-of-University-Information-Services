Biography

Dr. Liu Liu is an Assistant Professor in Finance. Prior to joining the University of Nottingham Ningbo China in January 2019, she was postdoctoral researcher at Research Center SAFE, Goethe University Frankfurt. Her research is mainly in the areas of asset pricing, macro finance, and financial econometrics.


Publications

  • Cho, S., Hyde, S. and Liu, L. (2018). 'The yen risk premium: A story of regime shifts in bond markets', Working paper.
  • Cho, S. and Liu, L. (2017). 'Correcting estimation bias in regime switching dynamic term structure models', Working paper.
  • Liu, L. (2017). 'Learning about the persistence of recessions under ambiguity aversion', Working paper.
