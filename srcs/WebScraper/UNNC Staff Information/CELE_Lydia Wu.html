Biography

Lydia joined CELE as an EAP [English for Academic Purposes] Tutor in 2020. Her work as a language teacher began in 2012. She has taught both English and Chinese languages at a variety of levels including secondary school, undergraduate, postgraduate and adult. As well as EAP, she has taught EFL [English as a Foreign Language], CFL [Chinese as a Foreign Language] and IELTS test preparation. At UNNC she has taught English to pre-sessional masters students and first year undergraduates. Her experience of teaching both English and Chinese has provided valuable insights which help her UNNC students to learn more effectively.
