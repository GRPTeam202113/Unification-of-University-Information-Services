Biography

I started teaching English in Tianjin Normal university where I received my BA in English Studies. In 2002, I decided to leave home for further education and different experiences. I spend a year in Scotland and received my Master degree at the University of Stirling. Since then, I have been teaching with Beijing Normal University, Tsing Hua University Zhuhai programme, HKU Global College in Suzhou before I joined the EAP team here at UNNC. I am currently the Preliminary Year Student Engagement Officer and I am also an advisor in ALDC(Academic Literacy Development Centre).


Teaching

Undergraduate:

- Learning English Acceleration Programme

- Listening and Speaking Improvement classes

- RWAC (Reading and Writing in Academic Contexts)

- LSAC (Listening and Speaking in Academic Contexts)

- ESAC (English in Specific Academic Contexts)

- AOP (Academic Oral Presentations)
