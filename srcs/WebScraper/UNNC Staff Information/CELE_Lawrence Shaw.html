Biography

I joined UNNC in October 2018. I have also taught EAP at other universities in China (Henan University of Technology, Zhengzhou), the UK (University of Southampton) and the Czech Republic (Technical University of Liberec). Previously, I worked predominantly in teacher training, as Course Director/Tutor on Trinity CertTESOL and other initial teacher training courses in the UK, Czech Republic, Spain and Italy. Overall, I have worked as a teacher and teacher trainer for the last 25 years.
