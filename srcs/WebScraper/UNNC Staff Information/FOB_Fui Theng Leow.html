Biography

Fui-Theng Leow (FT) has more than 14 years of teaching experience in international universities. Her teaching areas include media design, information systems, digital economy, management studies. Her research areas cover pedagogy and educational technology, health informatics, data analytics, social media and VR. Currently, FT also serves as the manuscript reviewer of several international journals, the lead of the Cultural & Creative Industries research priority group of IAPS, e-learning lead of the faculty, the faculty representative in the Digital Transformation Project, and the core member of V-ROOM project at University of Nottingham Ningbo China.
