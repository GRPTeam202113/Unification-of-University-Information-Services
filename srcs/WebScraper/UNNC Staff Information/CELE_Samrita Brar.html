Biography

I began working as an English instructor in 2006.  I taught preparatory EAP courses as well as general English courses at Mokpo National University in South Korea for four years.  Following that, I worked as an EAP tutor of general English at Guangdong Ocean University in Zhanjiang, China for 1.5 years.  Most recently, I taught in the intensive preparatory EAP program at Bilkent University in Ankara, Turkey for 3 years.
