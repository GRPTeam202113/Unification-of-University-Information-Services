Biography

I have been involved in English Language Teaching for over 20 years. I have taught, trained teachers, published practical and academic materials, and consulted on teaching and assessment materials in a variety of educational settings - from Kindergarten to Post-graduate!

Over the years, I have worked in several different countries, including China, Myanmar (Burma), the United Arab Emirates, Malaysia, Brunei Darussalam, South Korea, Thailand and, of course, my home country of Canada. I have published in teaching reference books, practical teaching publications, and professional websites, and delivered conference presentations, workshops and webinars to both students and teachers of English.
