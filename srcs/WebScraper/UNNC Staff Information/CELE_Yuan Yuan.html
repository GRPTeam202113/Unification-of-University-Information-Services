Biography

I started my ESL journey in 2014, teaching General English and Business English in Shanghai before moving to Beijing. From 2018, I worked exclusively as a teacher trainer on accredited qualifications for English language teachers in Shenzhen. Before joining UNNC, I worked as a Course Director of Trinity CertTESOL, Internal Assessor of Trinity DipTESOL and Lead Assessor of Trinity CertPT.

Teacher training courses (GTA, Outreach, Cambridge Professional Development Qualifications, NAA, etc)
