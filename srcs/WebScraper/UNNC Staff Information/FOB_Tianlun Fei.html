Biography

Prior to joining the NUBS China in September 2020, Dr Tianlun Fei was a teaching fellow in applied mathematics with School of Mathematical Science, University of Nottingham Ningbo China. His research is mainly in the areas of asset pricing and applied econometrics. He has taught a range of modules at undergraduate level, including calculus, numerical analysis, and programming such as Matlab and Python.
