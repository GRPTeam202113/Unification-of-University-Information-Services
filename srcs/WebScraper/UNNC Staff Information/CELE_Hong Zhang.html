Biography

Dr. Hong Zhang has been teaching in the Centre for English Language Education (CELE) at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC) since 2016 Sept. He is a CELE Content Tutor in Business and Economics, and Mathematics and Statistics for Business and Economics. He teaches courses for CELE PYP undergraduate students from NUBS. As a core member of teaching faculty for CELE PYP international foundation pathway, Hong specializes in developing and teaching mathematics and statistics for business and economics. He has successfully and independently designed and created two foundation pathway courses for CELE PYP: one is ‘Introduction to Mathematics for Business and Economics’ (module code: CELEF010), the other one is ‘Introduction to Statistics for Business and Economics (module code: CELEF009)’. Now, he is also the module convenor for 'Introduction to Business Economics' (CELEN042) as well as CELE International Business Team Leader since 2021.

Dr. Hong Zhang holds a bachelor degree in business management from University of Nottingham, a master degree with the distinction in accounting and finance from University of Durham, and a doctoral degree in finance from Nottingham University. Dr. Hong has a long-term commitment to both teaching and research – oriented HE career. His main research interests lie in corporate finance, corporate governance, financial reporting, Fintech, corporate innovation and so on. His recent research outputs have been published in world-leading journals in finance such as International Journal of Finance and Economics, International Journal of Financial Analysis. One of his academic articles on corporate governance was nominated for the 2018 best paper award by International Corporate Governance Society (ICGS). In the same year, he was awarded the UNNC 2017-18 Top Performer for the contributions to CELE PYP curriculum development as well as influential research outputs.
