Biography

I have taught ESP/EAP in China since 2016. Over the last 5 years I have worked with a variety of students (from young learners to young adults) to help them achieve their educational/academic ambitions. I joined UNNC in 2019 where I am able to apply my knowledge of Applied Linguistics to help teach academic writing and other EAP subjects.
