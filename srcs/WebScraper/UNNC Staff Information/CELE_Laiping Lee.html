Biography

I was raised in the post-industrial, post-Thatcher landscape of the north east of England. I studied art and then I left England. I have lived and taught in various countries over the years, France, Turkey, the UK and now China. One regret was not learning Chinese when I was younger, something which I am very very slowly trying to rectify. I have previously taught in a public university in Henan and also on pre-sessional courses run by the University of Southampton.
