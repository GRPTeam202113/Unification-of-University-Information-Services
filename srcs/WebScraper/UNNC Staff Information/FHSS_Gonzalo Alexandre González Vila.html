Biography

Alex González is currently working as a Language Tutor in Spanish in the Language Centre. Before joining the UNNC, he worked as a Spanish and English Lecturer in Henan Agricultural University, in Zhengzhou, China. Alex studied a master's degree in Linguistics Applied to the Teaching of Spanish as a Foreign Language at the University of Jaen, Spain. He had previously earnt a Bacherlor's and a Master's Degree in English Language and Literature, at the University of Vigo and University of Durham, where he was an exchange student for a year. In the school year 2013-2014 he earnt a Postgraduate Diploma in Education (Modern Foreign Languages) at the University of Sheffield, and also enjoyed teaching practice at different British high schools. Alex was the Exchange Coordinator for the Language Centre from 2019 to 2021, and became the Spanish Coordinator in 2020. Alex is specially interested in the teaching of linguistic and cultural aspects of the Spanish language.
