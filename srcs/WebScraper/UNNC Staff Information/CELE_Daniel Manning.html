Biography

Daniel has been a Course Tutor in English for Academic Purposes at the University of Nottingham Ningbo since July 2017 and has taught general and academic English at academic institutions in Europe, Asia, South America and the Middle East since 2008. During this time, he has developed professional experience in EAP, CALL, academic and creative composition, curriculum and assessment design, and the management and design of VLEs. He also has personal interests in creative writing, travel, front-end web design and morphosyntactic linguistics.

Daniel was promoted in the summer of 2018 to Co-convenor for ESACa International Studies and authored his first book titled 'A Student-Friendly Guide to Referencing' with Macmillan in September 2019 (a custom publication used throughout the Preliminary Year). He then took on a new role as Curriculum Development Team Leader in February 2020 and released the 2nd edition of his referencing guide to a global market in September 2020. Daniel became Senior Fellow of the Higher Education Academy in the summer of 2021 and is currently project leader for BALEAP institutional accreditation.
