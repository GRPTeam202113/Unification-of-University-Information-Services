Biography

I first started teaching English in 2003 at Beijing Language University. Since then, I’ve taught in South Korea, Oman, and the United Arab Emirates.

I’m interested in the role gender plays in language, particularly in mass media. I also enjoy learning foreign languages.

Outside of the classroom, I daydream about Anfield.
