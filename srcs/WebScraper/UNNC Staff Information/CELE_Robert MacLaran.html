Biography

My UNNC employment commenced in 2020, previously having worked in China for several years. This includes as an EAP Teacher for the VCE in China, working in collaboration with Haileybury School, Melbourne. In addition, I have delivered classes in General English, Reading and Writing; and, Pronunciation at Xinyang Normal University. Further, at Etherton Education, UK, I have taught several English pre-sessional courses for multilingual students proceeding to study GCSE and A-Level at British Boarding Schools. Here I successfully assisted the school to pass its British Council Accreditation.

My academic interests include pedagogy, learner motivation and etymology.
