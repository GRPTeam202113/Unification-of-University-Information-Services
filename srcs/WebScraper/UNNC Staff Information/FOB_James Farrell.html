Biography

Dr James Farrell is a Teaching Fellow in Economics in the Department of Finance, Accounting and Economics, in NUBS Ningbo. He was previously a lecturer in economics at Western Sydney. He currently teaches Introductory Economics and is First Year Officer for the School of Business.


Research interests

  • Macroeconomics
  • History of Economic Thought
