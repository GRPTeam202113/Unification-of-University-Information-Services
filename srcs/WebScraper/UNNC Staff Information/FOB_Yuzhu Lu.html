Biography

Before joining the University of Nottingham Ningbo China, Yuzhu has worked as Visiting Assistant Professor for two years in Lingnan University, Hong Kong, and one year as post-doctoral research fellow in Hong Kong Baptist University. She had teaching experience and interests in various subjects, including financial accounting, intermediate accounting, cost/management accounting, financial statement analysis, taxation, and corporate governance. Her current research lines in issues of determinants of corporate tax avoidance behavior, time trend in tax payment, and determinants and effects of corporate social responsibility activities.
