Biography

Ms. Han Yan is a visiting teaching fellow in finance and accounting at Nottingham University Business School (NUBS) China, University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC). She has a BSc degree in finance from Michigan State University in the United States, an MSc degree in marketing from the University of Bath, United Kingdom. Before joining Nottingham Ningbo, she worked in Ningbo Finance and Economics University as a lecturer in business administration. Her research lies in the areas of internet finance, crowdfunding, and finance and innovation. She has also worked as Principal Investigators for many research grants or consultancy programs of governments and research institutes.
