Biography

James has been teaching since 2008, during which time he has worked in Indonesia and Italy and around the UK in both private language schools and universities. Before coming to Nottingham Ningbo in 2017, he completed an MSC TESOL at the University of Bristol, where he wrote his dissertation on the use of spoken feedback techniques in one-to-one writing tutorials with students of English for Specific Purposes.
