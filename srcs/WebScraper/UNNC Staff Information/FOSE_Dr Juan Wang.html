Biography

After graduation, Dr. Juan Wang worked at the University of Nottingham, UK as a research fellow and a lecturer in geotechnical engineering. She is currently an associate professor in the Department of Civil Engineering, University of Nottingham Ningbo China, and the associate director of the Centre for Granular Materials and Geotechnology, NMI. Dr. Juan Wang was selected to join in Zhejiang Provincial ‘5246’ University Leading Talent Project and Ningbo Leading Talent Project.

Dr Juan Wang mainly conducts research in the field of transportation geotechnics. Her recent research areas include shakedown analysis theory and method for geo-structures, long-term performance of geomaterials and geostructures, low-carbon materials, functional road materials, utilisation of waste materials etc. Recently, she has been awarded several important research grants from government bodies and industrial partners. Moreover, she has successfully completed 8 external projects (around 11million RMB in total), published around 40 papers, obtained 3 patents, and successfully supervised several PhD students. She is currently an editorial board member of an ICE journal - Geotechnical Research, and also sits on two national technical committees in the field of Soil Mechanics and Geotechnical Engineering. She also serves as a reviewer for a number of renowned SCI journals. In addition, Dr Wang’s undergraduate course ‘Geotechnics 3’ was awarded as ‘Provincial Level First Class Undergraduate Course’.

Dr. Juan Wang is seeking for MRes and PhD students, and scholarship can be provided for excellent PhD students. You are welcome to contact her if you are interested.
