Biography

Yongmei Cai joined the University of Nottingham Ningbo China in September 2019 as an Assistant Professor in Applied Mathematics/Statistics. She received her BSc degrees from both Donghua University and University of Strathclyde through a 2+2 programme in 2015. Then she obtained her PhD in 2019, fully funded by China Scholarship Council, the MASTS pooling initiative (The Marine Alliance for Science and Technology for Scotland) and University of Strathclyde.


Teaching

 Statistical Models and Methods (MATH2038)

Stochastic population modelling / epidemic modelling
