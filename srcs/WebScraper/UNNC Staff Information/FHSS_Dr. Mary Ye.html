Biography

Mary YE (Ye Meng) is Teaching Fellow at UNNC. Mary has experience in both academia and the commercial world. Mary has the combined fields of corpus linguistic analysis, business communication and media discourse. Before joining UNNC, Mary taught technical and professional writing, English in hospitality and Language in Literature at UIC and Lingnan University. Further previously, Mary finished her PhD in Applied Linguistics at Department of English Hong Kong Polytechnic University. Right after her PhD studies, Mary joined a NY-based international knowledge brokerage company, working as Research Assistant/Associate for one year.
