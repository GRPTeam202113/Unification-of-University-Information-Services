Biography

Expertise Summary

Dr Yong SUN's expertise lies in Oil & Gas in both industry and academia, he has been active in prestigious Australian universities and Commonwealth research organization (Commonwealth Science and Industrial Research Organization-CSIRO) during the last ten years. He is interested in process intensification and industrial scale compact floating platform oil & gas processing, which involves with process intensification of industrial Fischer-Tropsch synthesis, cutting edge new generation reactor fabrications and optimizations, and machine learning for comprehensive kinetic modelling for Fischer-Tropsch synthesis.

Research Experience

  • 2018-present: Assistant Professor, Department of Chemical and Environmental Engineering, The University of Nottingham Ningbo China.
  • 2015-2018: Lecturer and senior engineer, Edith Cowan University, Australia.
  • 2011-2015: Scientist and process engineer, Australian Resources and Research Centre, Commonwealth Science and Industrial Research Organization (CSIRO), Australia.
  • 2010-2011: Postdoc Monash University, Australia.

Research interests

  • High performance catalyst preparation for Fischer-Tropsch synthesis
  • Cutting edge process intensification: microstructured reactor, fluidized bed reactor
  • Artificial neural networks and machine learning for comprehensive kinetic modeling   
  • Lithium battery recycling via hydrometallurgical process
  • Biomass high-valued conversion and biodiesel production
