Biography

Fangfang Hou is currently the PGCHE Convenor & PGR Trainer at Graduate School in The University of Nottingham Ningbo China. Her research interests include social media, e-commerce, digital innovation and intellectual property. She has published her research work in international journals, such as Production Planning & Control, Internet Research, and Industrial Management & Data Systems. She also presented her research and gave talks at international conferences. She was awarded the Tri-Campus Postgraduate Prize Awards by the University of Nottingham in 2017.
