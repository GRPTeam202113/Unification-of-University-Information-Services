Biography

Before joining the University of Nottingham in Ningbo China, I held various research positions in New Zealand. My main area of research is bio-mathematics, which is developing computational models for biological phenomena arising in ecology and medicine. I have also taught various undergraduate mathematics subjects at the University of Otago.
