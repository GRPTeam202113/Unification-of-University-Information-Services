Biography

Yimeng Wang is an architect, product designer and exhibition curator, graduated from Royal College of Art UK with RIBA part II certificate. With 7 Years’ top education in China, Denmark and UK, she works on different scale of designs from shopping mall to a piece of glasses with her own aesthetics.  Her focus is on critical thinking in both practices and teaching, inspired by religions / myth / metaphor / politics / economics etc. Her design wins red-dot award in 2017 and exhibit in Shanghai Biennial, her dissertation published in both China and UK.

Expertise Summary

Yimeng has strong ability to sublimate her teaching philosophy by her working experiences.


Teaching

As a practice-based tutor, Yimeng brings latest design strategies into studio teaching, with her design background and experiences, she could help students being open-minded and ambitious about architecture study.
