Biography

Chen joined the School of Economics in 2016 as an Assistant Professor at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China. He received his PhD in Economics from BI Norwegian Business School in 2016. His research focuses on game theory with applications and industrial organization. His current work is on iterated elimination procedures in normal-form and extensive-form games, and endogenous formation of economic coalitions in industrial organization. His work has been published in academic journals such as Economic Theory.
