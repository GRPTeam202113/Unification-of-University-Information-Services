Biography

Expertise Summary

Bingjian is a professional industrial designer and design academic. He received his BEng, MSc and PhD (Full Scholarship, Loughborough University) in the area of industrial design. He was the founding member of the department of industrial design at Xi’an Jiaotong-Liverpool University (XJTLU) and the Program Director of BEng Industrial Design. In September 2018, he joined Ningbo Nottingham University (UNNC) and acts as the course director of Product Design and Manufacture. Apart from design education and research, he also has close collaborative relationship with industry, including Hisense and CRRC which he designed the 500KM/H high speed train concept for in 2018.
