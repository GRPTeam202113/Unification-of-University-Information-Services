Biography

Prior to joining NUBS China, Dr Zhu completed her PhD in Business Administration at Richard Ivey School of Business, Western University. She focuses on individual-level entrepreneurship research, in particular, entrepreneurial decision making and its antecedents. Dr Zhu uses experiment and survey in her research and has published papers in ABS 3&4 journals.

Research interests

Research Summary

Dr Zhu is particularly interested in how psychological factors (e.g., emotions, motivations, identity) and family impact entrepreneurs and would-be entrepreneurs’ decision making. Dr Zhu is also interested in social entrepreneurship research.

Research Interests

Present & Future

  • Entrepreneurial persistence
  • Entrepreneurial decision making
  • Emotions in the entrepreneurship context
  • Social entrepreneurship
  • Family business succession
