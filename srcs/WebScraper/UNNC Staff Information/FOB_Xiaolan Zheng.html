Biography

Xiaolan Zheng is Associate Professor in Finance at the Nottingham University Business School China (NUBS China). She is the holder of the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA). Her research focuses on capital structure, debt maturity, dividend policy, cost of capital, trade credit, corporate governance, banking, international finance, corruption, and the influence of culture on financial decisions. She has published in the Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis, Review of Finance, Journal of International Business Studies, Journal of Corporate Finance, Journal of Banking and Finance,and Journal of Business Ethics. She currently teaches Business Finance at the undergraduate level and International Finance at the postgraduate level.
