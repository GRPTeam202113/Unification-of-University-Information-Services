Biography

Dr. Linjun Xie is Assistant Professor in the Department of Architecture and Built Environment at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC). She has a background in urban planning and her interdisciplinary research focuses on the governance of urban sustainability transitions. In particular, her work advances new ways of understanding urban sustainability initiatives and examines the reconfiguration of urban systems for resilient and just society. Till Feb 2022, she has published 19 articles in top peer-reviewed journals, including Political Geography, Journal of Urban Technology, Cities, Environmental Science & Policy, Journal of Cleaner Production, and Environmental and Planning E: Nature and Space.


Teaching

Linjun mainly teaches the MSc Urbanism and Sustainable Environment Program on areas of urban sustainability planning and governance. Modules include:

  • Sustainable Urbanism Project (ABEE 4082)
  • Eco– and Smart Cities (ABEE 4083)
  • Integrated and Innovative Environmental Design—Research Studio (ABEE 4085)

Research interests

Linjun’s research interests have covered: urban sustainability transitions, environmental governance, political ecology, and social and environmental justice. She has worked on two research projects on smart eco-city development and urban nature-based solutions:

  • 2016-2018: ESRC/NSFC/DFG/NOW/ANR co-funded research project – ‘Smart eco-cities for a green economy: a comparative study of Europe and China’ (http://www.smart-eco-cities.org/)
  • 2019-2021: EU Horizon 2020 Programme – ‘NATURVATION: Nature Based Urban Innovation’ project (https://naturvation.eu/)

Linjun has also been serving as a reviewer for international journals, including:

Sustainable Cities and Society; Ambio; Journal of Environment planning and management; China: An International Journal (CIJ); International Environmental Agreements: Politics, Law and Economics; Journal of Cleaner Production; Climate Policy; International Journal of Disaster Risk Science; Environmental Policy and Governance
