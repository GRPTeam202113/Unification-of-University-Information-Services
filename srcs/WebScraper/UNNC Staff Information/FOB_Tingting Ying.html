Biography

Tingting Ying joined the University of Nottingham Ningbo China in March 2018. She is the holder of the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) and has experience in certification training and education. She is also the editor for the IAFOR Journal of the Social Sciences and is a member of the editorial board of the International Journal of Service and Standards.


Teaching

  • Quantitative Methods
  • Microeconomics for Business

Postgraduate

  • Business Economics

Research interests

Tingting’s research interests focus on the inter-discipline of corporate finance and accounting, particularly corporate governance and market based accounting research in China, and also is interested in continuing research in a wider area subjects.

Research Interests
