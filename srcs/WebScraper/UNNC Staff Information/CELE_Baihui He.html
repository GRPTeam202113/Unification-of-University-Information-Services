Biography

I figured out that I wanted to teach when I was six or seven years old, and I even remember pretending to be a teacher to my childhood friends back then. With this dream, I chose to study at Northeast Normal University (NENU) for my BA, where I was well trained to be a teacher. After I graduated from NENU, I started my teaching career in a key public school. Before joining UNNC, I had taught middle school and high school students for more than eight years. I enjoy teaching, and I am proud to say that I have never stopped teaching, even while I was doing my MA.

In addition to teaching, I love walking a lot, particularly when the weather is cool and breezy. Walking is like communication between the walker and nature, and I can probably walk a whole day without feeling tired. My current schedule doesn't give me much time to walk, but I still try to walk a long distance when possible. If you are a walker, please invite me next time!
