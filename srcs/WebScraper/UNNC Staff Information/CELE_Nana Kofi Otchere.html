Biography

This is my second year teaching in the Centre for English Learning Education as a preliminary Science Tutor. A great motivator who is passionate about raising standards across all age groups and subject areas. Proficient at tailoring lesson plans to incorporate students’ diverse interests and capture their attention and imagination. With a natural air of authority is able to maintain discipline whilst maintaining a positive school atmosphere where students can reach their full potential.

With Qualified Teacher's Status, Kofi is an inspiring highly self-motivated charismatic educator, with vast experience in teaching all the Sciences in KS3-4 whilst specializing in Chemistry at AS level. Kofi has additionally taught in two prolific international schools in China, accumulating 5 years experience in delivering Cambridge and Edexcel syllabus at GCSE, AS & A Level.

Kofi has a knack for integrating everyday scenarios, analogies to assist students with the comprehension of scientific literature, concepts and ideas, with the aim of generating and stimulating independent and creative thinking. In so doing Kofi has been able to capture the attention and stimulated the creative thinking ability of students of diverse cultures, and EAL background. Kofi is a strong believer in equipping young people with life skills in addition to academia principles.
