Qualifications

Dr. Qing Xia is a Teaching Fellow in Finance in the Nottingham University Business School China. His main research interest is corporate finance, labor economics, venturing capital and innovation. Dr. Xia received a Bachelor in Finance from Xiamen University, MSc in Finance from Lancaster University and a Ph.D in Finance from Stockholm School of Economics.
