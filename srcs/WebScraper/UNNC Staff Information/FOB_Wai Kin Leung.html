Biography

Before Dr Leung joins the Nottingham University Business School China, he has taught at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, the University of Hong Kong and Louisiana State University, Baton Rouge, USA.


Teaching

Undergraduate

Financial Management, Investment and Real Estate Finance and Real Estate Valuation

Postgraduate

Financial Management, Investment and Real Estate Finance


Research interests

Dr Wai Kin Leung has published in top finance journals such as the Journal of Business, Journal of Futures Markets, Financial Research, Journal of International Financial Markets, Institutions, and Money, Real Estate Economics and Journal of Real Estate Finance and Economics. Dr Leung has won the 2006 Best Paper Award of the International Journal of Hospitality Management with a cash prize of US$3000. His paper, “Asymmetric Correlation and Volatility Dynamics among Stock, Bond, and Securitized Real Estate Markets” also won the Best Paper Award at the 2011 Global Chinese Real Estate Congress Annual Conference.

Research Interests

Investment, Derivative, Real Estate and Chinese Finance


Honorary positions

Dr Leung was member of the Executive Committee, School of Hotel and Tourism Management, the Chinese University of Hong Kong and Director-designate, Executive MBA Program in Real Estate Finance, the Chinese University of Hong Kong.
