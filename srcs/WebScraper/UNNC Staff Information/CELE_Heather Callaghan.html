Biography

Heather Callaghan has been a CELE Content Tutor in Mathematics at the University of Nottingham Ningbo (UNNC) since February 2020. She teaches on the UNNC Preliminary Year Programme (PYP) to undergraduate students on a variety of mathematics courses.

Heather Callaghan holds a Bachelor’s degree in mathematics, with modules in Operational Research (OR) and applications of mathematics and Master’s degree specialising in cryptography. She is currently completing a doctoral degree with Loughborough University in the UK in OR, specifically simulation modelling. Ms. Callaghan is committed to both a teaching and research-oriented career in higher education.

Research interests

Applications of Mathematics including Cryptography and the use of quantitative methods in Business and Economics. Operational Research – specifically how to integrate different research methods effectively.

Mathematics Education – how to improve understanding and logical thinking skills to apply mathematics to everyday situations.
