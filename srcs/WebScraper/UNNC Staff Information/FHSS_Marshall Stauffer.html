Biography

I have been in English education since 2010, teaching a wide variety of modules and content in the United States, Japan, and China. I’ve been with UNNC since 2013.


Teaching

Teaching

Advanced English Discourse Skills-module convener (post graduate), Reading and Writing in Academic Contexts (EAP), Listening and Speaking in Academic Contexts (EAP), International Studies (EAP), International Business (EAP), Academic Oral Presentations (EAP), Introduction to Academic Skills (EAP), Introduction to Analytical Thought (EAP) 

Programs & Roles

Currently involved in and oversee the CELE International Internship Programme; the Coaching & Support team (CAS) which provides in-sessional support and programs for Preliminary Year students; and the Academic Literacy Development Centre, which provides in-sessional support and programs for students from year two to post graduate.
