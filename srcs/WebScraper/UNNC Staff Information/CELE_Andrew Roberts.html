Biography

I’ve been working in the field of EFL/EAP/ESP for over twenty years and have lived and worked in the UK, the Middle East, Russia, SE Asia and China. I am an experienced English IELTS examiner, Teacher Trainer, ESP course designer/lecturer/author and international presenter.  For seven years I was a director/ partner in an International ESP company delivering training to governments and corporations across the globe.  I am married with three children and have a passion for cycling and the great outdoors. Some of the areas I’ve taught and designed courses for include:

DELTA local tutor, Aviation English ;Medical English; Business English - Report Writing, Meetings, Communication, Presentations; Legal English - Domestic and International Law; English for Finance; English for the Oil and Gas Industry; English for the Media; English for Socializing, English for the Power Industry. English for Senior Management
