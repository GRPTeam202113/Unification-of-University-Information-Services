Biography

Cass hold a BBA (Hons) in Finance from University of Tenaga Nasional, Malaysia and an MSc in Financial Management with Distinction from University of Greenwich, United Kingdom. She worked as Corporate Banking Officer in Bank of Tokyo-Mitsubishi, Malaysia before she furthered her study in London. After Cass obtained my master degree, she started to teach in University of Tunku Abdul Rahman, Malaysia. Cass then came to China and joined UNNC in year 2006.


Teaching

  • Introduction to Accounting
  • Seminar for Fundamental of Financial and Management Accounting

Research interests

Cass's research interests lie in the area of corporate finance. She is particularly interested in looking at what has determined the corporate financing decisions. Currently, Cass is also interested to study about the development of environmental issues and how will it influence the corporate in making financial decisions.
