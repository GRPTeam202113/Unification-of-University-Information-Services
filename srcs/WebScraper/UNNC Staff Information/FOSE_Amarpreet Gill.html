Biography

Expertise summary

Amarpreet Gill obtained his Masters of Fine Arts (MFA) in Transportation Design from Umea Institute of Design, Sweden. Upon graduating in 2011, he joined SAAB Cars AB, Sweden, as a Vehicle Interior Designer, until it unfortunately went into administration. Returning to the UK, he began working as a Freelance Designer and part-time lecturer at De Montfort University, Leicester, UK. Amarpreet has worked with several global companies such as ŠKODA Auto A.S. (Design Consultant), Enkai Custom Aquariums (Lead Designer), Money is the Root Clothing (Creative Director), Studio SDKZ China (Designer) and SAAB AB (Interior Designer). After a successful period as a freelance designer and part-time lecturer, he joined the Product Design and Manufacturing (PDM) team, in the Faculty of Science and Engineering at University of Nottingham, Ningbo, China (UNNC).

As a Design Studio Tutor, Amarpreet leads and convenes the sketching and rendering related modules and the design project modules in the PDM programme. Since joining UNNC, he has won over 30 international design awards, including Red dot, IF, IDA, European Design Awards and A’Design Award, secured several external funds and patents, and has published in international peer reviewed journals. In 2020, Amarpreet started his PhD in Education at UNNC, focusing on the use of mixed-reality and digital pedagogy in Design education and how they impact learning and academic performance.

Research interests

Amarpreet’s research interests span a number of areas, including technology-enhanced teaching and learning, Product/Industrial/Vehicle Design, Innovation Design and Creative Thinking, and Human-Computer Interaction. His PhD in Education focuses on the use of mixed-reality and digital pedagogy in Design education, and the impact on teaching, learning and academic performance.
