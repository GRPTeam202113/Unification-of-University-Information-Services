Biography

Dr.Tianxiang Cui is an assistant professor in the School of Computer Science at the University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC). Before joining UNNC, he was a senior AI engineer in Huawei and a senior algorithm researcher in PingAn. He was involved in some frontier industrial projects, including autonomous driving and quantitative trading. His main research interests include Computational Intelligence, particularly metaheuristic, evolutionary computation and neural networks; Machine Learning and Reinforcement Learning.
